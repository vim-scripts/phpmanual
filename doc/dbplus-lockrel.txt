==============================================================================
*Description*

    int dbplus_lockrel ( resource $relation )~

Requests a write lock on the given {relation}.

Other clients may still query the relation, but can't alter it while it is
locked.

==============================================================================
*Parameters*

{relation}

    A relation opened by |dbplus_open|.
