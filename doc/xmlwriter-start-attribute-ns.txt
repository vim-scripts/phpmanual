*Parameters*

    Only for procedural calls. The XMLWriter resource that is being modified.
    This resource comes from a call to |xmlwriter_open_uri| or 
    |xmlwriter_open_memory|.

{prefix}

    The namespace prefix.

{name}

    The attribute name.

{uri}

    The namespace URI.
