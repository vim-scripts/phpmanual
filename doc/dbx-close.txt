*Description*

==============================================================================
*Parameters*

    The DBX link object to close.

==============================================================================
*Return* *Values*

Returns 1 on success and 0 on errors.

==============================================================================
*Examples*

Example #1 dbx_close() example

<?php >
    $link = dbx_connect(DBX_MYSQL, "localhost", "db", "username", "password")
        or die("Could not connect");

    echo "Connected successfully";
    dbx_close($link);
<?>
