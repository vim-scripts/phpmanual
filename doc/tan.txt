==============================================================================
*Description*

    float tan ( float $arg )~

tan() returns the tangent of the {arg} parameter. The {arg} parameter is in
radians.

==============================================================================
*Parameters*

{arg}

    The argument to process in radians

==============================================================================
*Return* *Values*

The tangent of {arg}

==============================================================================
*Examples*

Example #1 tan() example

<?php >

    echo tan(M_PI_4); // 1

<?>

  * |deg2rad| - Converts the number in degrees to the radian equivalent
