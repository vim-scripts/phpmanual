*Description*

consists of the domains specified by {domlist} and is of type {type}

==============================================================================
*Parameters*

{relation}

    A relation opened by |dbplus_open|.

{domlist}

    A combination of domains. May be passed as a single domain name string or
    as an array of domain names.

{type}

==============================================================================
